Who is Ahmad Cj?

Ahmed CJ, is an engineering student, He is interested in design and programming, has been working in design for at least six years, with little programming experience. He is also interested in analyzing data and studying programming ideas and concepts. He currently lives in Sudan.

My Inspiration

In graphic design, it's hard to find what inspires you, but to me, the inspirational designers were the other designers who worked hard to create a wonderful image of their work. Everyone who worked hard also inspired me, In the end, it was the effort that made the work come out in a powerful, brilliant and clear way.

My Journey

I started my journey in general design by following films, as films represented a large and real part of my life, I have always tried to understand the nature of the composition of the arts and the scenes in general, of course there is no profound relationship between graphic design and film production, but after several experiments with specialized programs, I noticed that I like graphic design, which represents a way to express what I think or feel.
